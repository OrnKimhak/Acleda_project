Download the App

Steps to Download on iOS:

  1. Open the App Store on your iOS device.
  2. Search for "ACLEDA Mobile".
  3. Tap "Get" and authenticate if required (iOS have high security then android).
  4. Wait for the installation to finish, then tap "Open".

Steps to Download on Android:

  1. Open the Google Play Store on your Android device.
  2. Click on Search button".
  3. And you will see this display on your screen.
  4. And then search for "ACLEDA Mobile.
  5. When you finished the download you can just click on Open to run the app.

Sign Up

  1. Open the ACLEDA Mobile app.
  2. Select "Sign Up".
  3. Fill in your details.
  4. Create and confirm a password.
  5. Tap "Submit".

How to register ACLEDA mobile

  1. Open "ACLEDA mobile".
  2. Choose "ALLOW" buttons".
  3. Select language
  4. Select ACLEDA Bank's logo
  5. Enter your phone number to register and press the arrow
  6. Select "Allow While Using App"
  7. Please read general terms and select "ACCEPT" button
  8. The system will automatically verify your phone number
  9. Select document for register
  10. Please put your face in the camera box, blink eyes and smile and choose "OK" button
  11. Select "OK, I'M READY" button and Take photo of your identity document
  12. Please check your registration information and fill in the information correctly with your identity document and choose "OK" button
  13. Input your new 6-digit PIN and enter new PIN again and choose "OK" button
  14. Select "OK" button
  15. Your registration was successful
    Please select the answer to this question and Select "START BANKING NOW" button
  16. Please choose "Accounts" menu to see your wallet accounts
  17. You can see your wallet accounts as registered phone number in KHR and USD
