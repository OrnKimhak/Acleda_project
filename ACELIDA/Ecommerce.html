How to pay your E-commerce bill

1. Open " ACLEDA MOBILE " app

2. select " PAYMENT " to menu

3. Use Touch ID, Facial, or enter PIN to login

4. Select "E-Commerce" menu

5. Choose e-commerce company/website

6. Fill in your payment information correctly

7. Choose "OK" button

8. Verify your payment information

9. Choose "CONFIRM" button

8. Your payment was completed
