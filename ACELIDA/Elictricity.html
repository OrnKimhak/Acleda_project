How to pay electricity bill of private company

1. Open " ACLEDA MOBILE " app

2. select " PAYMENT " to menu

3. Use Touch ID, Facial, or enter PIN to login

4. Select "Electricity" menu

5. Choose "Private Electricity

6.1 Fill in your payment information correctly Debit account Bill number Payment amount

6.2. Choose "OK" button

7.1. Verify your payment again

7.2. Choose "Confirm" button

8. Your payment was completed
