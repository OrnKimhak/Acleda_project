How to Transfer Money to Acleda Account or Phone Number

1. Open " ACLEDA MOBILE " app

2. select " TRANSFER " menu

3. Use Touch ID, Facial, or enter PIN to login

4. Select "ACLEDA Account | Phone Number" menu

5. Enter transfer information (select debit account, input recipient's phone number, & transfer amount)

5.1 Press "OK" button

6. Verify your information

6.1 Press "CONFIRM" button

6. Your transaction is completed

In case the recipient's phone number is not registered yet, you will receive a gold code to be given to the recipient to withdraw money.
