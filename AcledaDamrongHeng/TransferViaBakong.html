How to Make Transfer Money to Bakong Wallet

1. Open " ACLEDA MOBILE " app

2. select " TRANSFER " menu

3. Use Touch ID, Facial, or enter PIN to login

4. Select "LOCAL TRANSFER" menu

5. Choose "TRANSFER TO BAKONG ACCOUNT"

6. Enter transfer information

6.1 Enter Amount

6.2 Press "OK" button

7. Verify your transfer information

7.1 Press "OK" button

8. Your transaction is completed
