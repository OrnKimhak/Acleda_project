How to mobile top-up (pinless)

1. Open " ACLEDA MOBILE " app

2. select " MOBILE TOP-UP " menu

3. Use Touch ID, Facial, or enter PIN to login

I.Pinless

4. Select "Source Account"

4.1 Enter "Mobile Number"

5. Verify your top up information

5.1 Swipe to "confirm" your top up

6. Your transaction is completed

The system will top up your phone number automatically
