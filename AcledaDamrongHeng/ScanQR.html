How to Make Payment with QR Code

1. Open " ACLEDA MOBILE " app

2. select " Scan QR " menu

3. Use Touch ID, Facial, or enter PIN to login

4. Please align frame with QR code or press "Select QR" if you have QR photo in your phone menu

5. Enter payment information (select debit account and input payment amount)

5.1 Press "OK" button

If you need to add tip, please click on Tips icon

6. Verify your payment information

6.1 Press "CONFIRM" button

6. Your transaction is completed
