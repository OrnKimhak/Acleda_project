How to register ACLEDA mobile

1.1 After downloading ACLEDA mobile in your phone app

1.2 Open " ACLEDA MOBILE "

2 Choose " ALLOW " buttons

3 Select language

4. Select ACLEDA Bank's logo

5.1 Enter your phone number to register

5.2 Press the arrow

SIM card must be in this phone

6. Select " ALLOW While Using App"

7.1.Please read general terms

7.2. Select "ACCEPT"button

8.The system will automatically verify your phone number

9. Select document for register

10.1. Please put your face in the camera box, blink eyes and smile

10.2. Choose " OK"button

11.1. Select OK, I'M READYbutton

11.2. Take photo of your identity document

You have to use valid and origional document

12.1. Please check your registrarion information and fill in the information correctly with your identify document

12.2. Choose " OK"button

13.1. Input your new 6-digit PIN and enter new PIN again

13.2. Choose " OK"button

If the system cannot verify your information, there will be further check.

Select "OK"button

14.1Your registration was successful

14.2Please select the answer to this question

14.3. Select " START BANKING NOW"button

15. Please choose "Accounts"menu to see your wallet accounts

16. You can se your wallet accounts as registered phone nnumber in KHR and USD
