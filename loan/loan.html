How to request loan

verifiedRequest your loan or loan against term deposit of ACLEDA Bank on ACLEDA mobile conveniently.

  1. 1.1. Open "ACLEDA mobile" app
  2. 1.2. Select "Loans" menu
2. Use Touch ID, Facial, or enter PIN to login
3. Select "Request Loan"
  1. 4. Choose "Request"
  2. The purpose of completing this loan application is to request the credit officer to study and prepare the loan documents at your place, which is not a type of digital loan to receive money immediately.
  1. 5.1. Enter required information
  2. 5.2. Press "OK" button
  1. 6.1. Verify your request information
  2. 6.2. Press "CONFIRM" button
  1. 7. Your request is completed
  2. After receiving your request, our officer will contact you to confirm the transaction.
